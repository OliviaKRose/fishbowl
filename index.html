Setting up a fish tank

  1. First off a tank - minimum size required is around 20L
  2. Then you'll need pick a substrate, gravel or sand are both popular
  3. Next you'll need to choose your decorations/hardscape
  4. Now it's time to add some life in the form of plants
  5. Not quite time for fish yet - you need to wait for your tank to "cycle"
  6. Finally, time to add some fish!

My fish

Orinoco

Orinoco is a mini Snowball Pleco and he'll reach a maximum size of 10cm (other pleco species can grow up to 2ft long). He's named after the river his species is native to in Venezuela (although there's also a womble named Orinoco)

The tetras

The green neon tetras were the first fish in the tank, I have a school of seven of them. They like to hide out in the plants at the back

Black bee shrimp

The shrimp are the busiest in the tank, they make a very efficient clean up crew and are the chillest dudes ever

Kuhli loaches

The newest tank additions, they are still quite shy

Snails

There is a never ending supply of pond snails in the tank so I hired some assassins to manage the population
